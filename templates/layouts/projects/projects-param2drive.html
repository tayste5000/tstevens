Param2Drive

param2drive uses the Biopython library to compute some common chemical properties of a protein from a user-submitted sequence. This information is then formatted into a text document and inserted into the users google drive service. The project was designed with the goal of encouraging biochemistry lab members to store this information in a way that is easy for other lab members to access.

Click here to use param2drive.(note: you will be prompted to give the site permission to access your google drive account before you can use param2drive)
